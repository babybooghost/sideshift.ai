What it does

Verify

Catch the hallucination

An independent cross-model critic scores the claim, tags every sub-claim, and cites honestly. No invented sources. Flip on web grounding for real live citations.

ELI5

Break the jargon wall

Highlight a term and get a two-sentence plain-English definition in the exact context you are reading.

Diff

Stop re-reading whole files

Highlight code, ask for a fix, and get a minimal git-style diff instead of a full reprint. Code is auto-detected.

Merge or discard

No context pollution

Close a tangent for good, or fold a short summary into the thread so a side-quest is never lost.

Minimap

Never lose the thread

Minimize side-chats to glowing nodes on the edge, then click to jump back to what you highlighted.

Your key

Local by default

Bring your own Anthropic or OpenRouter key, encrypted on your machine. No server sees your screen or your prompts.

See it in the flow

Highlight. Pick an action. Answer in place.

Press the hotkey over any app, drag a box over the text or code, and a translucent side-chat streams the reply. The main thread never moves. Move it with the keyboard, hide it with a keystroke, dock it to the edge.
